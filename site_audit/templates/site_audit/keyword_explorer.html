How to use this tool

Look-up the keyword you are writing about in Google and see what types of articles already rank well.

This should give you an idea of what to write about. For instance, if you Google “raw honey” you will see a lot of articles in the top 10 positions talking about the “benefits” of “raw honey”. It is a good idea to follow their example! Also localize your article if you are focused on a specific city or area. For instance, it is easier to rank for “How to find a dog walker in New York City” than the national term “Dog walker”.
